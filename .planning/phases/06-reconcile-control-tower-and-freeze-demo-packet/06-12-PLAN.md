- The dashboard homepage no longer accumulates stale active-surface child-page links after repeated page replacement cycles.
- Dashboard sync can recognize an unchanged page even when preserved child pages/databases remain embedded.
- No plan/run/QA/gap semantics change; this is strictly a P6 control-plane drift fix.
